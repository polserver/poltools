<B>Section 1: Definitions</B><BR>
<BR>
<U>1.1 Player</U><BR>
A person who has access to an Ultime Online account and using it to play a character shall henceforth be known as a player.<BR>
<BR>
<U>1.2 Staff</U><BR>
Volunteers who include Counselors, Seers, Game Masters, Administrators and Developers easily identified by their job title in front of their name. Such volunteers shall henceforth be known as staff.<BR>
<BR>
<U>1.3 Harassment</U><BR>
Abuse, threatening or otherwise violating the legal rights (such as rights in privacy and publicity) of others. Publishing, posting, uploading, distributing or disseminating any inappropriate, profane, defamatory, infringing, obcsene, indecent or unlawful topic, name, material or information shall henceforth be known as harassment.<BR>
<BR>
<U>1.4 Non Player Character (NPC)</U><BR>
A character generated that is run on scripts by the server and not actively controlled by a player shall henceforth be known as an NPC.
<BR><BR>
<U>1.5 Player Killer (PK)</U><BR>
The attacking of another player, willing or not, shall henceforth be known as PK.<BR>
<BR>
<U>1.6 Res-Killing</U><BR>
The death of another player a lot of time in a short period and caused by players, willing or not shall henceforth be known as res-killing.<BR>

<B>Section 2: General Rules</B><BR>
<BR>
<U>2.1 Harassment</U><BR>
Harassment is strictly prohibited. Violating this rule may result in permanent termination of your account.<BR>
<BR>
<U>2.2 Paging</U><BR>
Staff is available at all times of the day to aid you with any problems or concerns you may have. Do not page with threatening pages, profanity or obscene and graphic language.<BR>
<BR>
<U>2.3 Bugs</U><BR>
Cheating or deliberately using game bugs to disrupt the game is considered a violation of the rules. If you learn a bug, notify the staff immediately.<BR>
<BR>
<U>2.4 Banned Accounts</U><BR>
Do not allow players who have been banned from Fantasia to access your account. Such a violation will result in the permanent termination of the account.<BR>
<BR>
<U>2.5 3rd Party Tools</U><BR>
Using Ghost Mouse & UOXLoop are officially authorized. Razor is accepted ONLY when you train. It is strictly forbidden to use Razor for PvP and PvM.

<B>Section 5: Chain of Punishment</B><BR>
<BR>
<U>5.1 Minor Offenses</U><BR>
You will be notified of what you have done wrong and/or disconnected from Fantasia and issued a warning that shall be stored in your account file. Repeat offenses will get you jailed for a period of time between one and fourth days and shall receive another warning stored in your account file.<BR>
<BR>
<U>5.2 Medium Offenses</U><BR>
You will be jailed for a period of time between one and fourth days and shall receive a warning stored into your account file. Repeat offenses will get you jailed for a period of time between fourth and nine days and shall receive another warning stored into your account file.
<BR><BR>
<U>5.3 Serious Offenses</U><BR>
Particularly serious violations can result in extended jail time or permanent termination of your account, without warning.<BR>
